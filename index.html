✨ Choose Your Wizard ✨

Enemy wizard will be chosen at random from the legends.

⚡ WIZCARD · Wizard Duelling Guide ⚡
📜 Core Rules
  • Each wizard has 50 Health and starts with 6 Mana. Gain 6 Mana at the start of each turn (max 12).
  • You always have 7 cards in hand. After playing a card, immediately draw one from your deck (unless you gained a card by other means).
  • Reduce the opponent wizard's Health to 0 to win.
  • The battlefield is a 3x3 grid. Front, middle, and back rows determine attack order and targeting.
  • Each turn: play any number of cards → wizard attacks → all creatures attack → end turn.
🃏 Card Types
  • 🔥 Spell — Deal damage, apply status effects, heal, or summon special effects.
  • 🐉 Creature — Summon to the board. They attack each turn and may have passive abilities.
  • 👓 Equipment — Provide lasting buffs or powerful one‑time effects.
  • 🤫 Secret — Trigger automatically when conditions are met, consuming mana.
  • ✨ Token — Copy cards generated by abilities or specific cards. They do not go to the discard pile.
⚡ Elemental Reactions (Core Combo Mechanic)
🔥 Burnt + 💧 Soaked → 4 direct damage (to target wizard or creature)
💧 Soaked + ⚡ Shocked → 2 damage + Stun (skips next attack)
⚡ Shocked + 🔥 Burnt → 2 damage and splash 1 damage to other enemies

※ When both statuses are present, the reaction triggers immediately and removes both.

🏆 Champion Abilities
Harry Potter — Spells costing 3 or less are cast twice (same target).
Hermione Granger — After casting a spell, reduce the mana cost of a random spell in your hand by 25% (rounded down).
Ron Weasley — When summoning a creature, grant a random permanent buff: +1 ATK / 25% double attack / +2 HP.
Albus Dumbledore — Every fourth spell you cast gives you a token copy with half mana cost. 5% chance to summon a Phoenix when casting a spell.
Gellert Grindelwald — Every 6 cards played summon an Acolyte. Your spells cost 1 less mana but cost 1 HP. Acolytes repeat spells by spending HP equal to the original cost. Ally deaths trigger Protego Diabolica.
Vinda Rosier — Damaging the enemy wizard has 25% chance to inflict Fear (+1 mana cost to a random hand card). Killing any enemy gives 2 mana and Vindictiveness (+1 damage next attack). After dealing 20 total damage, summon an Acolyte.
Luna Lovegood — Every 5 mana spent summons a random beast. Every 10th summon summons an additional Thestral. You can always see Thestrals (no Spectrespecs needed).
Newt Scamander — When you summon a non‑token creature, gain a free Niffler token in hand.
Minerva McGonagall — When you cast a spell, 40% chance to transfigure a random enemy creature into a Teacup (1 HP, 0 ATK). If no enemy creatures exist, cast Piertotum Locomotor instead.

※ Some abilities track hidden counters (spell count, mana spent, etc.). Hover over the wizard tile to see progress.

🌀 Status Effects Quick Reference
  • Disarm — Cannot attack or cast spells next turn (affects wizard/creature attacks).
  • Stun — Skips next attack for creatures, and gain 3 mana instead of 6 next turn for wizards.
  • Bleed — Takes 1~2 damage at end of turn.
  • Heal — Restores 1~2 health at end of turn.
  • Fear — A random card in hand costs +1 mana for 2 turns.
  • Unseen (Thestral) — Cannot play Thestral unless you wear Spectrespecs or are Luna.
  • Runic Ward — Absorbs the next instance of damage.
  • Obscurus / Obscurial — Transforms the wizard, doubling damage taken but also doubling attack power.
🎮 Controls & Tips
  • Click a card in hand to select it → click a flag on the board (valid targets are highlighted) to play it.
  • Click empty space or outside the board to cancel selection.
  • Hover over any card, creature, or wizard to see detailed description and current status.
  • The "Retreat" button on your creatures returns them to your deck and gives +1 mana immediately.
  • Elemental reactions are key to turning the tide. Try to combine low‑cost elemental spells.
  • Each champion's special counters (e.g., Dumbledore's spell count, Luna's mana spent) are shown when hovering over the wizard tile.
